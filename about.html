ABOUT

I’m a visual storyteller who loves to capture the small, fleeting moments that make everyday life special — whether it’s during my daily routine or while traveling and exploring new cultures. Over the years, I’ve cultivated my own photographic style and artistic voice, shaped by curiosity, emotion, and an eye for detail. Most recently, I’ve been working with a company specializing in portrait photography of kindergarten children, creating warm and genuine portraits for parents to cherish. You can purchase my photographs in your preferred size through the store or gallery — feel free to take a look around. And if you have any questions or would like to collaborate on an upcoming event or campaign, I’d love to hear from you.
